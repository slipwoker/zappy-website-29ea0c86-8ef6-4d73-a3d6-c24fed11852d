    Frequently Asked Questions

    How far in advance should I book?

    We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance, especially during peak season. For honeymoon couples and luxury wellness travelers planning extended stays, we suggest booking 1-2 weeks ahead to ensure your preferred time slots are available.

    What should I bring to my appointment?

    We provide everything you need for your wellness spa experience, including towels, robes, and slippers. Simply bring yourself and an open mind. If you have any specific skincare products you prefer, feel free to bring them.

    Can I combine multiple treatments?

    Absolutely! Many guests at Hesa Wellness Spa Ubud combine treatments for a comprehensive wellness experience. Our packages like The Elements Journey are designed to maximize the synergistic benefits of multiple treatments.

    Is there parking available?

    Yes, we offer complimentary parking for all guests. Our facility is easily accessible from central Ubud, with clear signage to guide you to our tropical sanctuary.

    Do you accommodate couples?

    Yes! Hesa Wellness Spa Ubud is a popular choice for honeymoon couples. We can arrange side-by-side treatments and create romantic experiences that make your visit truly special.

    What is your cancellation policy?

    We understand that plans can change. Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid any charges. For group bookings or special packages, different terms may apply.
